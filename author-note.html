author’s note

thank you everyone for reading all the way to the end.

your love and support has meant the world to me. thank you for sticking around, for the messages, for caring about these characters as much as i do.

this was never meant to be a happy story. it was never going to have a happy ending. because the truth is you can’t save others, and love will not save you, that’s the reality.

a lot of young women are fed the illusion of the knight in shining armor. the family, the marriage, the idea that the right person will fix everything and a lot of men are burdened with unhealthy, unreachable expectations of what they’re supposed to be. these illusions are what end up hurting people: putting women in abusive situations, or worse. you might not have agreed with reader’s choices throughout the story, but they’re common, they’re relatable to a lot of young women who have been there.

my goal was never to write strong characters. it was to write human ones.

my view on life might be a little pessimistic, lol.

i’m not sure what comes next for crying during sex. maybe AUs, maybe one shots, who knows but i’d love to hear your thoughts and feelings about the story now that it’s complete.

and a huge thank you to my friend michelle for helping with the voice acting, and to ethel cain for the inspiration

— angel
